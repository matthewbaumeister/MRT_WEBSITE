# Make Ready Brand Guide Reference

## Brand Colors

### Primary Purple
- **HEX**: `#2F2F72`
- **RGB**: R 47, G 47, B 114
- **CMYK**: C 99, M 98, Y 20, K 8
- **Usage**: Primary brand color, headers, main elements

### Gold Gradient
- **Gradient Angle**: -138 degrees
- **Colors**: Metallic gold with highlights
- **HEX Approximation**: `#D4AF37` (base gold)
- **Usage**: Accent color, CTAs, highlights

### Supporting Colors
- **Dark Blue/Navy**: Used in logo, text
- **White**: Text on dark backgrounds
- **Light Gray**: Backgrounds, subtle elements

---

## Typography

### Font Family: **Paralucent**

#### Title
- **Style**: Extra Light
- **Tracking**: 15
- **Case**: Title Case
- **Usage**: Page titles, hero headlines

#### Header
- **Style**: Medium
- **Tracking**: 0
- **Case**: Title Case
- **Usage**: Section headers, page headers

#### Subhead
- **Style**: Bold
- **Tracking**: 50
- **Case**: ALL CAPS
- **Usage**: Section labels, category titles

#### Body
- **Style**: Light
- **Tracking**: 10
- **Case**: Sentence case
- **Usage**: Paragraph text, descriptions

## Logo Specifications

### Primary Logo
- **Symbol**: Geometric "M/MA" shape
  - Upper portion: Dark blue (#2F2F72)
  - Lower portion: Metallic gold with gradient
- **Text**: 
  - "MAKE": Dark blue, stacked on top
  - "READY": Metallic gold, stacked below
- **Orientation**: Vertical stack

### Banner Logo (Horizontal)
- **Variation 1**: Symbol left, "MAKE READY" right (mixed colors)
- **Variation 2**: "MAKE READY" left (all blue), symbol right

### Symbol ("Pentagon")
- Standalone geometric mark
- Available in multiple color variations:
  - Blue and gold (standard)
  - White and blue on dark background (reversed)
  - Blue on gold background
  - Outlined versions for each

- Sharp edges (not rounded)

## Brand Voice

- **Professional**: Government contractor tone
- **Confident**: Expert, capable, reliable
- **Veteran Values**: Service, integrity, mission-focused

## Application Guidelines

### Buttons
- Primary: Purple background, white/gold text
- Secondary: Gold background, purple text
- Hover: Add gold accent/border

### Headers/Navigation
- Dark purple or dark navy background
- White text with gold accent on hover
- Logo in upper left (white/gold version on dark)

### Cards/Components
- White backgrounds
- Purple headers
- Gold accents for emphasis
- Subtle shadows

### Footer
- Dark purple/navy background
- White text
- Gold for links/hover states

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
✅ Use brand purple and gold consistently  
✅ Maintain logo proportions  
✅ Use approved fonts (Paralucent)  
✅ Keep designs clean and professional  
✅ Emphasize veteran-owned status  

### DON'T:
❌ Alter logo colors or proportions  
❌ Use unapproved colors  
❌ Add effects to logo (no shadows, outlines, etc.)  
❌ Rotate or skew logo  
❌ Place logo on busy backgrounds

## Notes for Web Development

- Primary purple is darker than previous (#2F2F72 vs #6B46C1)
- Gold should have gradient/metallic appearance where possible
- Font: Paralucent (will need to license or use web-safe alternative)
- Logo files needed: PNG and SVG formats
- Consider dark mode with reversed logo/colors

## Font Alternative (If Paralucent Not Available)

If Paralucent font cannot be licensed:
- **Alternative 1**: Montserrat (similar modern sans-serif, Google Fonts)
- **Alternative 2**: Inter (clean, professional, Google Fonts)
- **Alternative 3**: Work Sans (geometric, similar feel, Google Fonts)

Current site uses Inter - can be replaced with Paralucent when licensed.
